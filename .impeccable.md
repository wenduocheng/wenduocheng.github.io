# Design Context

## Users
**Target audience:** Academic collaborators, potential advisors, recruiters, fellow researchers in computational biology and AI.

**Context:** Visitors are evaluating Wenduo's research credentials, looking for collaboration opportunities, or assessing fit for academic/industry positions. They're scanning for publications, research interests, and getting a sense of who Wenduo is as a researcher and person.

**Job to be done:** Quickly assess research quality and focus areas, understand Wenduo's unique contributions to computational biology and AI, and feel confident reaching out for collaboration or opportunities.

## Brand Personality
**Voice & Tone:** Professional and authoritative, yet collaborative and open. Energetic and ambitious without being overwhelming.

**3-word personality:** Collaborative, Ambitious, Rigorous

**Emotional goals:**
- Inspire confidence in research capabilities and scientific rigor
- Convey approachability and openness to collaboration
- Communicate forward-thinking innovation in AI × Biology
- Show personality and humanity alongside academic achievements

## Aesthetic Direction
**Visual tone:** Add more visual interest while maintaining professional authority. Move beyond generic academic templates toward a distinctive, memorable presentation.

**References:** Inspired by contemporary academic sites (yingsiqin.github.io, sjunhongshen.github.io, zhengyiluo.com) that balance:
- Clean, minimalist layouts with generous whitespace
- Visual thumbnails/GIFs for research projects
- Personal touches that humanize the academic format
- Modern typography and intentional color use
- Dark/light mode support
- Clear visual hierarchy with section separation

**Theme:** Light mode primary (current), with potential dark mode consideration for future enhancement.

**Anti-references:**
- Generic Jekyll academic templates that all look identical
- Overly busy, cluttered layouts
- Pure text-heavy pages with no visual breaks
- Overly formal, impersonal presentation

## Design Principles

1. **Visual Engagement Over Pure Text:** Use visual elements (color, typography hierarchy, potential for project thumbnails) to break up dense academic content and guide the eye.

2. **Clarity With Character:** Maintain professional credibility while injecting personality through intentional design choices—distinctive typography, thoughtful color use, personal sections.

3. **Hierarchy Drives Understanding:** Clear visual hierarchy helps visitors quickly find what they need: research at the top, credentials supporting, personality adding depth.

4. **Breathing Room:** Generous spacing prevents cognitive overload, making dense publication lists and technical content more digestible.

5. **Collaborative Spirit:** Design should feel open and inviting, reflecting Wenduo's collaborative personality—approachable rather than intimidating.
